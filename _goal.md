# 
- Assess the security posture of an enterprise environment and recommend and implement **appropriate security solutions**.
- Operate with an awareness of applicable regulations and policies, including principles of governance, risk, and compliance.
- **Monitor** and secure hybrid environments, including cloud, mobile, and Internet of Things (IoT).
- **Identify, analyze, and respond** to security events and incidents.

#
| Feature | Governance | Risk Management | Compliance |
|--------|------------|-----------------|------------|
| Primary Goal | Alignment and Strategy | Uncertainty and Resilience | Integrity and Rules |
| Question it asks | “Are we doing the right things to **reach our goals?**” | “What could go wrong, and **how do we handle** it?” | “Are we **following the laws** and internal rules?” |
| Key Action | Setting policies and deciding who is accountable | Identifying, assessing, and mitigating threats | Monitoring and auditing activities against standards |
| Focus | Strategic (Top-down) | Analytical (Predictive) | Tactical (Historical/Check-based) |
| Example | Creating a policy on data privacy and ethical AI use | Running a scan to find security gaps in your cloud storage | Passing an audit to show you follow GDPR or SOC 2 rules |
